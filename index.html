Welcome

I am an oceanographer seeking to better understand our Earth system, applying mathematics and engineering principles in the process. I am particularly interested in oceanic mesoscale and submesoscale flows, both in the upper ocean and deep sea, and their influence on turbulence. In particular, I am interested in how these small-scale processes might impact energy and buoyancy budgets in the ocean. My chief tool to do so is to use observations, but I have recently become interested in complementing these observations with numerical models to better answer these questions.

Within these pages, you'll find an overview of my research, a list of people with whom I work, a few publications and links I think you might find useful. In the future, I intend to host a few pictures.

Please flip through the pages of this website and email me (right) if you have any questions.
