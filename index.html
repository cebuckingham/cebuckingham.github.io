Welcome

I am interested in oceanic submesoscale flows. In particular, I am interested in how physical processes that occur at these small horizontal (0.1-10 km) and temporal (hours to days) scales impact energy and buoyancy budgets in the ocean and, ultimately, climate. I use satellites, moorings and ship-based measurements to answer these questions.

Within these pages, you'll find an overview of my research, a list of publications and links you might find useful. Additionally, in the future, I will host a few pics.

Please flip through the pages of this website and email me (right) if you have any questions.
