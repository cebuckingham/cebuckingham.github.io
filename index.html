Welcome

I am an oceanographer applying mathematics & physics principles to improve our understanding of the Earth climate system. I am particularly interested in submesoscale flows in the upper ocean and deep seas, and how these might impact large-scale ocean circulation. My chief tool for doing so is the examination of observations, but I have recently become interested in complementing these observations with numerical models to better answer these questions.

Within these pages, you'll find an overview of my research, a list of people with whom I work, a few publications and links I think you might find useful. In the future, I intend to host a few pictures.

Please flip through the pages of this website and email me (right) if you have any questions.
